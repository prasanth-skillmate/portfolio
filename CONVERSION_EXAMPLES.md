## IMPLEMENTATION PRIORITY

1. **Start with Hero Sections** - Highest visibility, biggest impact
2. **Update CTAs** - Easy win, immediate improvement
3. **Add Social Proof** - Builds trust quickly
4. **Simplify Language** - Makes content more accessible
5. **Add Risk Reversal** - Removes hesitation

---

## QUICK REFERENCE: Conversion Principles

1. **Clarity > Cleverness** - Simple, clear messages convert better
2. **Benefits > Features** - Focus on what visitors get, not what you do
3. **Social Proof > Claims** - Show, don't tell
4. **Risk Reversal > Sales Pitch** - Remove risk, make decision easy
5. **Action > Information** - Every section should lead to action

---

**Remember:** Test one change at a time, measure results, then iterate.
